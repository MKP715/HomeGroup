| 1:20 | 10 | 16 | **Larry J. & Houston (1940)** | The man who brought AA to Texas with only a Big Book, first Houston meeting (Mar 15, 1940), first AA pamphlet ever published (Houston Press), "The Texas Prayer" (Apr 1, 1940), cautionary lesson of iron-hand leadership |
| 1:30 | 10 | 17 | **The False Start: Kent W. (1941–42)** | Ruth T.'s first inquiry letter (Jun 14, 1941), Kent W. as "Chief Sponsor," White Plaza Hotel meeting (Jan 7, 1942), 72 inquiries then total collapse, interactive comparison table: promotion vs. attraction, table talk discussion |
| 1:40 | 15 | 18 | **Esther E. — A Flower of the South** | New Orleans background, wedding bourbon story, Saturday Evening Post article (Mar 1, 1941), sobriety date (May 16, 1941), March 29 letter to New York, **April 2, 1943: The Dallas Group founded**, Ruth T. present, meetings Tues/Fri at 8 PM, fewer than 20 sober by 1945, inscribed Big Book at Central Office |
| 1:55 | 10 | 19 | **Post-War Growth: Searcy W. & Suburban Group** | Searcy W. sobriety (May 5, 1946), Suburban Group founded (Sep 1946), first decentralization to neighborhoods, offspring groups (Preston, Belmont, etc.), Central Office opens (Sep 18, 1947), Dick P. and Jake Leg, growth stats (2→8→30→160+) |
| 2:05 | 10 | 20 | **Ebby Thacher & the Traditions** | Searcy reads handwritten Traditions on airplane (1948), "We don't need this," 1950 Convention adoption, Ebby Thacher's story — Bill's sponsor saved in Dallas (1953), found on Bowery, sober at Suburban Club, reflection discussion |
| 2:15 | 10 | 21 | **Fort Worth, NETA 65 & the Bigger Picture** | George McL. (1941), Ralph the zealous 12th-Stepper, organizational evolution (Central Office → Intergroup → Dallas AA Central Office), NETA 65 structure (29 districts, 512+ groups), District 22 connection |

## Afternoon Roundtable Topics (2:45 – 3:30 PM)

Each table selects or is assigned one topic. A facilitator guides 30 minutes of discussion, then the table prepares a 2-minute summary to share.

| Table | Topic | Guiding Questions |
|-------|-------|-------------------|
| **1** | Welcoming Newcomers | How does your home group welcome someone at their first meeting? What could improve? |
| **2** | Service: Getting Involved | How do we encourage members to take on service? What makes service attractive vs. burdensome? |
| **3** | Group Conscience in Practice | How does your group handle business meetings? What makes a group conscience "informed"? |
| **4** | The GSR & District Connection | Does your group have a GSR? How does information flow between your group and District 22? |
| **5** | Challenges & Solutions | What is the biggest challenge your home group faces? What Tradition speaks to it? |
| **6** | Sponsorship & the Home Group | How does your home group support sponsorship relationships? What could improve? |
| **7** | Keeping Meetings Fresh | How do you prevent meetings from becoming stale? What formats work best? |
| **8** | Home Group as Family | What does it mean that the home group is our "extended family"? How do we live this out? |
